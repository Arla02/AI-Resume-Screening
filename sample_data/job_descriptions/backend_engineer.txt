Backend Software Engineer

About Us:
We are a fast-growing fintech startup building the next generation of payment infrastructure. Our platform processes millions of transactions daily for clients worldwide.

The Role:
We're looking for a Backend Software Engineer to join our core platform team. You'll be working on our payment processing systems, building scalable APIs, and ensuring high reliability of our financial services.

Requirements (Must Have):
- 3+ years of experience in backend software development
- Strong proficiency in Python
- Experience with Django or Flask frameworks
- Solid understanding of REST API design and implementation
- Experience with relational databases (PostgreSQL preferred)
- Familiarity with Docker and containerization
- Good understanding of software testing practices
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience

Nice to Have:
- Experience with microservices architecture
- Knowledge of message queues (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
- AWS experience (EC2, S3, Lambda)
- Experience in fintech or payment processing
- Contributions to open-source projects

Responsibilities:
- Design, develop, and maintain backend services and APIs
- Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code
- Participate in code reviews and technical design discussions
- Collaborate with frontend engineers and product managers
- Troubleshoot and resolve production issues
- Contribute to improving our engineering practices

What We Offer:
- Competitive salary and equity
- Health, dental, and vision insurance
- Flexible work arrangements
- Learning and development budget
- Regular team events and offsites

Location: San Francisco, CA (Hybrid - 3 days in office)
Salary Range: $140,000 - $180,000 + equity
